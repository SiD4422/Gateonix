Faculty Mentor · Associate Professor
Dr. S. Usha
Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering
SRMIST, Kattankulathur
Dr. S. Usha received B.E in Electrical and Electronics Engineering and M.E in Power Electronics and Drives from Anna University in 2007 and 2012 respectively. She completed her Ph.D from SRM Institute of Science and Technology in 2020. She has published more than 78 international and national journals and 10 Patents. Her research interests include Power Electronics Converters, Stability analysis of Induction motors, and Electric drive systems.
Power Electronics Machine Design Stability Analysis Inverter Design Electric Drives

Dr. A. Geetha
Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering
SRMIST, Kattankulathur
Dr. A. Geetha received B.E in Electrical and Electronics Engineering and M.Tech in Power Electronics and Drives in 2009 and 2012.
